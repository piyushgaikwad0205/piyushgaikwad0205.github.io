Club under Student Club

These student clubs offer limitless opportunities for student leadership and participation beyond classroom setup and academic programme.

Home Clubs

Dance Club

Welcome to our dance club, where the beats never stop and the energy is always high. We provide our patrons with an unforgettable nightlife experience, featuring top DJ's and music that will keep you moving all night long. Our club is the perfect place to let loose and have fun with your friends. We pride ourselves on providing an atmosphere that is safe, fun and welcoming to everyone.

Music Club

At Music Club, we are dedicated to being a musical hub for emerging artists. Our stage is a showcase for raw, undiscovered talent waiting to be discovered by the world. We strive to provide an intimate setting for music lovers to connect and enjoy the performance together. Our team works tirelessly to create a warm, welcoming atmosphere that is perfect for a night out with friends.

Drama Club

Welcome to the Drama Club, where creativity and performance meet. Our club is open to everyone who loves acting, regardless of their experience level. We offer a variety of programs that cater to all ages and skill levels, from beginners to advanced actors. Our team of experienced instructors provides personalized coaching and feedback to help each member reach their full potential.

Gaming Club

At Coding Club, we believe that coding is more than just a skill. It's a mindset that requires dedication and passion. That's why we work tirelessly to provide students with the resources they need to build their coding skills, as well as opportunities to network and collaborate with other like-minded individuals. With an abundance of workshops, events, and other learning experiences

Coding Club

At Coding Club, we believe that coding is more than just a skill. It's a mindset that requires dedication and passion. That's why we work tirelessly to provide students with the resources they need to build their coding skills, as well as opportunities to network and collaborate with other like-minded individuals. With an abundance of workshops, events, and other learning experiences.

Robotics Club

Our robotics club is dedicated to fostering innovation and creativity in robotics. We provide a fun and challenging environment where students can learn new skills, develop their talents, and explore new technologies. Our team is always working on exciting new projects, from designing and building robots that can navigate obstacles to programming complex algorithms.

VSA Club

Visual and Arts Club is a creative community of filmmakers who love the art of reels making. Our mission is to provide an environment where members can learn new techniques, collaborate with like-minded people, and most importantly, have fun. Our goal is to create a platform where individuals can express themselves through creative storytelling and explore the limitless potential of reels making.

Creative Club

At Creative Club, we believe that everyone has an artistic side waiting to be explored. That’s why we provide a welcoming space where creative individuals can come together to collaborate and share their talents in various forms of art. Our organization’s primary goal is to foster a sense of community amongst members to build lasting connections and discover new talents.

Nexus Club

At Nexus Club, we are committed to providing a supportive community that empowers women to lead fulfilling and successful lives. Our goal is to promote personal wellbeing by providing a safe and inclusive space, as well as offering a variety of resources and services that address the unique challenges faced by women. Joining the Nexus Club means entering a community of like-minded individuals dedicated to uplifting and celebrating one another.

Fitness Club

Our fitness club is designed to offer accessible and enjoyable ways for both students and staff to lead healthy and active lifestyles. Our range of state-of-the-art facilities, experienced trainers and variety of group fitness classes ensures that there is something for everyone. We believe that fitness should be a part of your everyday life, and we offer a supportive and inclusive community that can help you achieve your goals.

Event Club

At Event Club, we believe that student engagement is the key to creating a vibrant, fulfilling college experience. That's why we are dedicated to organizing a broad range of social, cultural, and academic events throughout the academic year. We strive to bring together students from all walks of life to connect, interact and celebrate together. So, if you want to make the most of your time at college.

Publicity Club

The Publicity Club at our college is dedicated to promoting student events and experiences on campus. Our team of passionate and creative individuals works tirelessly to raise awareness and generate enthusiasm for all the exciting events that take place. We specialize in utilizing various channels to reach out to the student body and beyond, from social media to traditional advertising.
