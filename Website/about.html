About Student Club :

Student Clubs at a college are attuned to help students get most out of their experience while they are enrolled at the college for their academic pursuits

With 16 different clubs, students get to explore and grow their talents by performing on various stages and conducting events. These student clubs offer limitless opportunities for student leadership and participation beyond classroom setup and academic programme.?

Vision And Mission

Vision: To create a common platform for every student of college to enhance, nurture and showcase their talent.

Mission: To provide opportunities and encouragement to the students to follow their passion and enhance their abilities.
